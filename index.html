About Washy Washy World

Your trusted laundry service in New Bedford

Our Story

Washy Washy World was founded with a simple mission: to provide the New Bedford community with clean, affordable, and convenient laundry services.

We've created a welcoming environment with state-of-the-art equipment to make your laundry experience as pleasant and efficient as possible.

Whether you prefer self-service, drop-off, or pick-up and delivery, Washy Washy World is here to serve you.
